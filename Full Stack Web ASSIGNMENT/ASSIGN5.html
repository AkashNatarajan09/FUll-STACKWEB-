My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Inception
    • Year: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page
    • Description: A mind-bending thriller about a thief who enters the dreams of others to steal information, but is tasked with planting an idea in someone's mind.
  2. The Dark Knight
    • Year: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
    • Description: A gritty and intense superhero film where Batman faces off against his greatest foe, the Joker, in a battle for Gotham's soul.
  3. Interstellar
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Actors: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain
    • Description: A visually stunning science fiction epic about a team of astronauts traveling through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.
  4. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
    • Description: A powerful drama about a man wrongly imprisoned who forms an unbreakable friendship and ultimately finds redemption.
  5. Forrest Gump
    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright, Gary Sinise
    • Description: A heartwarming story about a man with a low IQ who, despite his challenges, lives an extraordinary life and influences some of the most significant moments in history.
